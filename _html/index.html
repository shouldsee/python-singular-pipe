class singular_pipe.types.Prefix(*a, **kw)¶

Bases: singular_pipe._types.PrefixedNode

The definition of a Prefix is unclean. The expansion of Prefix cannot takes place until the upstream job is done.

For example, a tar archive job may wants to monitor all files under prefix ./build

This node is useful when specifying a loosely defined fileset into LoggedSingularityCommand()
